Laser Source

A Continuum Integra-C femtosecond Ti:Sapphire amplifier delivering ultrashort pulses for high-precision micromachining of thin films and nanostructures.

Wavelength
790 nm (near-infrared)
Pulse duration
130 femtoseconds
Max pulse energy
1.5 mJ per pulse
Repetition rate
1 kHz mode-locked
Beam profile
TEM00 Gaussian, linear polarization
Power control
Automatic attenuator (0.01–1.0 mJ)

Power Measurement

Inline power monitoring via a Thorlabs PM103 interface with S120VC silicon photodiode sensor, calibrated at 790 nm (certificate #26147114921, 2026-05). A 1:1000 beam splitter samples 0.1% of the laser beam to the sensor.

Interface
Thorlabs PM103 (optical power meter)

Thorlabs S120VC (Si photodiode, 200–1100 nm)
Power range
50 nW – 20 mW (at sensor, 450–1100 nm band)

Accuracy
±1% (AO2), ±3% (sensor, 440–980 nm)
Calibration
DIN EN ISO 9001, all 21 ranges PASS

35.9 mA/W (EEPROM auto-read)
Controller readout
