Notes

- This instrument is used for time-resolved gas-sensing Raman experiments.
- The 600 l/mm grating gives a wider spectral range per acquisition but
  lower resolution compared to the 1200 l/mm grating on the Wasatch system.
- The confocal hole (150 um) provides depth resolution for film measurements.
- Detector temperature of -75 C gives lower dark current than the Wasatch
  system (-15 C), improving SNR for weak Raman signals.
- Time-series data is stored as: header + wavenumber row + N intensity rows
  (one per time point). Use extract_timeseries.py to split into individual
  2-column spectra compatible with the WO3_GO_Raman_pipeline.
